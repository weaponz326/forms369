Data Collection & Automation

Forms369 automates 90% of the processes involved in filling and submitting forms.

Keeping an Up-to-date User Profile

Forms369 keeps a comprehensive profile for all respondents or clients. During registration, the details provided by a client is added to the user profile. A user also has the opportunity to manage their profile: edit, delete and add more details to their profile.

Forms Auto-Complete

The process of filling a form is fully automated. When filling a form, all details from the respondent's profile are used to autocomplete the form. As the user profile grows, the forms autocomplete process becomes more accurate, saving respondents an enormous amount of time.

Data Auto-sync

When filling a form, respondents have the opportunity to edit data in autocompleted field, as well as fill out uncompleted fields. All these changes: editted and new respondent's data from the form filling process are automcatically synched into the client profile, thus maintaining an up-to-date user profile.

High End Security and Data Protection

You data is highly secured and protected. At GiT we recognize the importance of protecting your personal information and are committed to processing it responsibly and in compliance with applicable data protection laws in Ghana and other countries GiT will operate. With forms369, companies only have access to respondent's data provided in filled forms.

Two-way Authentication

We use two way authentication: Login and SMS Verification to keep respondents' data secured.

One of the ways Forms369 keeps its users data secure is by implementing a two-way authentication. After successful login via username and password, a verification code is sent to the users SMS. To fully login and have access to the functionalities of this application, a usr must pass all the 2 stages of authentication.

Flexibility and Forms Originality

Print Data in the Original Form Format

With Forms369, when creating a form, you can also upload the original document of the form that is being created in PDF format. Once a form has been created, published and filled, you have the opportunity to print the original form document with respondents' data. This helps to maintain the originality of the form while making it easy to gather respondents data.

Forms Creation

Forms creation made easy with already created Templates and Sections

Form Templates

To streamline the form creation process on Froms369, several form templates have been created. During form creation, the creators can pick a template that suit their needs from the pool of templates. This chosen template can be editted to include all needed fields.
